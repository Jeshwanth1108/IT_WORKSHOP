\[ x^n + y^n = z^n \]
The formula of
\[(a+b)^2\]
\begin{equation}
a^2+b^2+2ab=(a+b)^2
\end{equation}
\[(x+y)^2\]
\begin{equation}
x^2+y^2+2xy=(x+y)^2
\end{equation}
This is a simple math expression \(\sqrt{x^2+1}\) inside text.
And this is also the same:
\begin{math}
\sqrt{x^2+1}
\end{math}
but by using another command.
This is a simple math expression without numbering
\[\sqrt{x^2+1}\]
separated from text.
This is also the same:
\begin{displaymath}
\sqrt{x^2+1}
\end{displaymath}
